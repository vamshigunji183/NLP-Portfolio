About Yuan Huang

Hi, I'm Yuan Huang, a theoretical physicist and an aspiring data scientist.

I am completing my PhD from University of Massachusetts, Amherst and expect to graduate in the fall of 2017. In my PhD research, I study strongly correlated quantum materials using Quantum Monte Carlo methods in order to better understand their magnetic properties at low temperature.

As a Data Science Fellow at Insight, I am really excited to apply my coding and analytical skills to the rich data from real life and solve challenging problems with a great impact.
